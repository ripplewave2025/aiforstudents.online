How we work with students

1) Simple rules

  • Short sessions (60s). One question at a time.
  • Ask for steps, examples, and a quick self-quiz.
  • Children use it with an adult in the room.

2) Build thinking, not copying

  • We rewrite in the student’s own words.
  • We practice, check, and reflect (“what changed?”).
  • We celebrate effort over instant answers.
